## Executive Summary (Verbatim Canonical Inclusion)

This repository documents a fixed, irreversible digital material lifecycle system
modeled on real-world physical transformation and entropy.

The system is not an investment product, yield mechanism, financial instrument,
or governance system. It defines a predetermined lifecycle in which a single
fungible digital material may be irreversibly committed, passively decay, and later
be exited into a permanent historical artifact.

The system does not promise appreciation, rewards, access, or future utility.
It records irreversible actions and preserves their outcomes as immutable history.

Any meaning, value, or cultural significance that develops is emergent and external,
not guaranteed or engineered.
